About MaungaClub

Only Club members can access the chalets. • All chalet bookings must be from Friday to Sunday. • Chalets offer shared accommodation for between 6 and 40 people per chalet. • All three chalets are on the mountain, so you can ski to and from the chalets. • The mountain gives 550 hectares of skiing/snowboarding area, and the longest ski/snowboard season in New Zealand.
